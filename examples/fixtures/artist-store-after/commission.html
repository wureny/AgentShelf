Commission process

Example Artist Studio accepts a limited number of custom calligraphy and hand-painted object commissions. Buyers provide the gift occasion, recipient context, requested word or name, deadline, shipping country, and budget range. The studio confirms feasibility before payment. Typical production takes 10 to 15 business days after details are confirmed. Custom work can be canceled within 24 hours, but cannot be returned after production unless damaged in transit.
