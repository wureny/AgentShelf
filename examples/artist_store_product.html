Moon Kiln Calligraphy Teacup

Moon Kiln Studio makes handmade ceramic teacups with custom Chinese calligraphy for gift buyers, tea lovers, wedding shoppers, and collectors who want a one-of-one artist-made object. Each cup is thrown, glazed, and painted by the artist in small batches. The teacup is best for meaningful cultural gifts, tea rituals, and display pieces where the story matters as much as the object.

Material: glazed ceramic. Capacity: 180ml. Customization: buyer may request one short name, phrase, or blessing in Chinese calligraphy. Production time is usually 10-14 business days before shipping. Ships to the United States, Canada, and the United Kingdom with protective packaging and tracking.

Price: USD 128. Made to order. Because each piece is custom, cancellations are accepted within 24 hours of purchase and returns are reviewed case by case if the item arrives damaged. Care: hand wash only; do not microwave.
