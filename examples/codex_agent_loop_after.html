Moon Kiln Calligraphy Teacup

This page is about Moon Kiln Calligraphy Teacup, a made-to-order handmade ceramic teacup from Moon Kiln Studio for gift buyers, tea lovers, wedding shoppers, and collectors. It is best for meaningful cultural gifts and tea rituals where a one-of-one artist-made object is more important than mass production. The teacup is 180ml glazed ceramic, can include one short Chinese calligraphy name, phrase, or blessing approved before firing, and is made in small batches by the studio artist.

Price is USD 128. The item is available for custom order, with production usually taking 10-14 business days before shipping. Moon Kiln Studio ships to the United States, Canada, and the United Kingdom with protective packaging and tracking. Cancellations are accepted within 24 hours of purchase; damaged arrivals are eligible for return review within 7 days with photos. Care instructions: hand wash only and do not microwave.
